Calendar Android App made to suply a personal necessity.

This app was made using GeminiAI

![ReminderCalendar](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a4e39cce-0a4e-4589-8398-33247b870bc8)

## Features
- **Let you configure a message to sent to the configured contact via: SMS, Mail or WhatsApp.**
- Month, week and day view.
- Uses Google Account to sync between devices.
- Material Design.
- Dark and light theme.
- Header, button and day indicator color can be customize.

## How to use ReminderCalendar
- Choose a calendar from your phone to sync to and from.
- Customize the reminder message you would like to send.
- Choose the desired send method.
- Create an event by clicking on the hours list and set the event name and contact information.
- Hit the **bell** that appears next to the event to **automatically** open the desired app with your message ready to send.

## Important permissions that requires
- READ_CONTACTS (optional)  
  - Is requiered to import contact information from your phone so name, phone and mail can be filled automatically. 
- READ_CALENDAR & WRITE_CALENDAR  
  - Is requiered to sync the events so you can't lose it and to work with multiple devices if google account is configured.
